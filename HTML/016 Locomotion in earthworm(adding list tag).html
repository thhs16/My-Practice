The main aspects of locomotion in "Earthworm"

  • Circular muscles
  • Longitudinal muscles
  • setae
  • fluid filled in the coelom




   Steps of locomotion

  1. In the anterior end, the circular muscles contract resulting the relaxing longitudinal muscles and the setae lose contact with the ground.

  2. The head end extend or move forward.

  3. At the head region, the circular muscles relax and the longitudinal muscles contract.Then, the setae in that region become to touch the ground.

  4. The body region behind the anterior end is pulled forward by the help of anchoring setae.
